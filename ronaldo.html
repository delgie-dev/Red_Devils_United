The Story

From Madeira to Old Trafford

Born in Funchal, Madeira on February 5, 1985, Cristiano Ronaldo showed extraordinary talent from childhood. After excelling at Sporting CP's academy, an 18-year-old Ronaldo dismantled Manchester United's defence in a pre-season friendly, prompting Sir Alex Ferguson to sign him on the spot.

Ferguson signed him for £12.24 million — one of the greatest bargains in football history. Ronaldo was handed the iconic No. 7 shirt, previously worn by legends including George Best, Bryan Robson, Eric Cantona, and David Beckham.

What followed was one of sport's great transformations: a flashy but inconsistent teenager grew into the most complete forward on the planet, winning United's first Champions League in nine years.
