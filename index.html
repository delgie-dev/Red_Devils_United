🏟️ Old Trafford

The Home

Old Trafford

Old Trafford has been Manchester United's home since 1910. With a capacity of 74,310, it is the largest club football stadium in England and one of the most iconic in the world.

Nicknamed the "Theatre of Dreams" by Sir Bobby Charlton, Old Trafford has witnessed some of the most legendary nights in football — European glory, title-winning seasons, and the roar of 74,000 devoted fans.
